About Us

A Web app which is developed for testing the traits of a candidate named as "Personality Traits Assessment", by using Deep Learning Models. Models are trained to prediact Big Five traits from a video of a candidate.
the Big Five traits includes

  • Openness
  • Conscientiousness
  • Extraversion
  • Agreeableness
  • Neuroticism
